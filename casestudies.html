Case Study - 1

Enhancing Crop Yields with Smart Irrigation

A group of farmers in Bihar faced challenges due to erratic weather patterns and water scarcity. They adopted Hariyalikart's Smart Irrigation System, which helped them optimize water usage based on real-time data. This resulted in a 30% increase in crop yields and a significant reduction in water costs.
